# 🎨 Logo Design Prompts for RailAspirant App

## App Overview
- **Name**: RailAspirant
- **Purpose**: Railway exam preparation and revision questions
- **Target**: Students preparing for Indian Railway exams (RRB, NTPC, ALP, Group D, JE)
- **Languages**: 15 Indian languages (multilingual education)
- **Platform**: Android & iOS mobile app

---

## 🏆 RECOMMENDED PROMPT (Modern & Professional)

```
Create a modern, professional mobile app logo for "RailAspirant" - an Indian railway exam preparation app.

Design elements:
- Incorporate a stylized train or railway track symbol
- Integrate an open book or graduation cap to represent education
- Use India's tricolor inspiration (saffron, white, green) or professional blue/orange
- Modern, clean, minimalist design suitable for app icon
- Should work well at small sizes (512x512px, 192x192px, 48x48px)
- Flat design or subtle gradient
- Icon should be recognizable even without text

Style: Modern, professional, trustworthy, educational, aspirational
Format: Square app icon with rounded corners
Colors: Professional blue (#1E88E5) with orange accent (#FF6F00) OR tricolor inspired
Typography: Bold, modern sans-serif for "RA" monogram if included

The logo should inspire confidence and aspiration in railway exam students.
```

## 📐 TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Required Sizes for Play Store & App Store:
- **High-res icon**: 1024x1024px (PNG, no transparency for iOS)
- **Android adaptive icon**: 512x512px (foreground + background layers)
- **Play Store feature graphic**: 1024x500px
- **Various densities**: 192x192, 144x144, 96x96, 72x72, 48x48px

### Design Guidelines:
- ✅ Simple and recognizable at small sizes
- ✅ Works in both light and dark modes
- ✅ No fine details that disappear when scaled down
- ✅ Avoid text in icon (except monogram)
- ✅ Safe area: Keep important elements in center 80%
- ✅ Test on different backgrounds (white, black, colored)
